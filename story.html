Behind the Stitches

Empowering Artisans Across India

The Hands That Create

Every crochet toy and flower at Phool is more than just a beautiful product; it is a story of resilience, skill, and empowerment. We partner directly with over 500 women artisans from the remotest villages across India.

For generations, these women have passed down the intricate art of crochet and knitting. Phool provides them with a platform to share their heritage with the world, transforming a traditional household skill into a sustainable livelihood.

Sustainable Practices

We are deeply committed to protecting the environment that inspires our floral designs. All our yarns are sourced ethically, prioritizing premium organic cottons and natural dyes.

By working from their own homes, our artisans reduce their carbon footprint and maintain a healthy work-life balance, allowing them to care for their families while achieving financial independence.
